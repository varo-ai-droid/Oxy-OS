## Your Task
Write the "Why this exists" founder video script (60-90s). Authentic, not marketing.

### Tone
- Vulnerable, specific, not polished
- "I built this because I lived this pain"
- Talk to ONE store owner

### Structure
1. **The Moment** (10s): Specific day you felt the pain
2. **The Failure** (20s): What you tried that didn't work
3. **The Insight** (15s): What you realized about the problem
4. **The Build** (15s): What you made, why it's different
5. **The Invitation** (10s): "If this is you, come check it out"

### Example (Counter Brief style)
> "Hey, I'm Nate. Last Black Friday, I watched 3 chargebacks hit my store in one hour. The tool I paid $200/month for sent the bank a generic template. All three lost. That's $4,200 gone.
>
> I looked at what the bank actually needed: proof the item shipped, proof the customer got it, proof they were happy. My store HAD all that - tracking, emails, order history. But nobody was assembling it.
>
> So I built Counter Brief. It pulls the real evidence from your store and builds the exact packet the bank wants to see. You read it. You approve. $19 flat. You keep everything you win.
>
> If you're losing chargebacks you should win, come check it out. CounterBrief.com."
