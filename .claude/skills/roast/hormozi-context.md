# Hormozi Framework Context (Updated: 2026-07-05)

**Value Equation** — (Dream Outcome × Likelihood) / (Time × Effort) = Value. Increase dream outcome/likelihood, decrease time/effort for perceived value.

**Grand Slam Offer** — An offer competitors can't match. Combines unmatchable value proposition, premium price, unbeatable guarantee, attractive promotion, and money model.

**Starving Crowd** — Market with massive urgent demand. Demand > offer strength > persuasion skills. Find people already spending money on suboptimal solutions.

**Money Models** — Four monetization layers: Attraction (get attention), Upsell (increase value), Downsell (alternatives), Continuity (recurring). Fix weakest link first.

**Dream Outcome** — What the prospect desperately wants. Focus on status outcomes: be beautiful, respected, powerful, loved. Sell outcome, not features.

**Done-For-You** — Eliminate prospect's effort/sacrifice. Liposuction vs. gym: same outcome, vastly different perceived value due to effort differential.

**Customer Avatar** — Detailed profile beyond demographics. Include pain points, desires, beliefs, objections, and behavioral patterns for precise targeting.

**Scarcity/Urgency** — Limit quantity or time to increase perceived value. Psychological solution that works better than logical improvements.

**Bonuses/Guarantees** — Value stacking and risk reversal. Bonuses should eclipse core offer value; guarantees make people feel stupid saying no.

**Rule of 100** — 100 actions/day, 100 days to build lead generation momentum. Non-negotiable for solving lead problems.

**Customer Financed Acquisition** — Use customer revenue to fund acquisition. Three levers: CAC↓, LTGP↑, PPD↑.

**Core Four** — Four essential lead generation methods: Community, Events, Content, Referrals. Test all four, double down on what works.

**Coaching Workflow** — Seven-step process: Diagnose → Prescribe → Sequence → Sprint → Review → Iterate → Scale. Apply to any business problem.
