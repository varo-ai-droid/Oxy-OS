# Researcher

## Definition
Entity group for researchers and scientific contributors to exercise physiology and strength training.

## Notable Figures
- [[C. Klein]] - Bronze/silver era transitional figure
- [[Joe Weider]] - Founder of IFBB
- [[George Hackenschmidt]] - Early 20th century physical culture
- [[Chuck Sipes]] - Golden era bodybuilder
- [[Piri Radar]] - Iron Man magazine founder

## Significance
Historical researchers and coaches who developed training methodologies before modern scientific validation. Their programs provide insight into effective training practices independent of anabolic assistance.
