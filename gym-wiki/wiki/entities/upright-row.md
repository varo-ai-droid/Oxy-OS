# Upright Row

## Description
A vertical pulling exercise where the barbell is pulled upward along the body to chin level, primarily targeting the upper trapezius and medial deltoids. The movement is similar in muscle activation to the [[Vertical Shrug]] but with additional scapular upward rotation.

## Form Cues
- Pull barbell along the body, keeping it close
- Lead with elbows, raising them to shoulder height
- Avoid excessively wide grip which can cause shoulder impingement
- Control the eccentric phase

## Variations
- Dumbbell upright row - Allows neutral grip position
- Cable upright row - Provides constant tension throughout the movement
