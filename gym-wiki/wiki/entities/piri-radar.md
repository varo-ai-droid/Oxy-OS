# Piri Radar

## Description
Founder of Iron Man magazine and influential figure in both silver and golden era bodybuilding. Authored extensively on advanced training methods including isometrics, rest-pause, and cluster-style training.

## Contributions
- Founder and longtime editor of Iron Man magazine (highly influential in silver/golden eras)
- Early advocate of specialization phases in bodybuilding
- Wrote about advanced methods including:
  - Isometric training
  - Rest-pause training
  - Cluster-style set structures

## Notable Programs
- **Leg Specialization Phase** (1955, Ironman Bible Course Book):
  - 6 exercises performed 3x/week within full-body framework
  - Flat foot barbell squat
  - Stiff leg deadlift (multiple sets)
  - Heels elevated squat
  - Used ~10 rep range with double progression

## Historical Significance
- Bridged theoretical knowledge gap between silver and modern training approaches
- Documented varied training methods that predated scientific validation
- Represented transition toward more specialized bodybuilding programming
