# Iron Man Magazine

## Description
A bodybuilding and fitness magazine founded by Piri Radar in 1936. Iron Man magazine was influential in publishing training programs and concepts from the silver and golden eras of bodybuilding.

## Historical Significance
- Published many silver era training programs and routines
- Advocated for specialization phases and focused training blocks
- Documented training approaches from pre-steroid era bodybuilding
