# Bill Pearl

## Description
Golden era bodybuilder (1930s-1960s) who represented the transition period into the early steroid era. Unlike many of his contemporaries, Pearl was known for his natural approach to bodybuilding and his comprehensive training knowledge.

## Historical Significance
- Represented the transition between silver and golden era training philosophies
- Known for his comprehensive exercise knowledge and programming
- His training approaches bridged the pre-steroid and steroid eras of bodybuilding
- Documented training methods that influenced later generations
