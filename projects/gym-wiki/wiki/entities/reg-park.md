# Reg Park

## Description
Mr. Universe winner and influential golden era bodybuilder known for his strength-focused training approach and popularization of the 5x5 training system. First bodybuilder to bench press 500 lbs naturally.

## Historical Significance
- Won Mr. Universe title (1958, 1965)
- First bodybuilder to achieve 500 lb bench press without anabolic assistance
- Bridge between silver era (pre-steroid) and golden era training philosophies
- Influenced later bodybuilders including Arnold Schwarzenegger

## Training Contributions

### The 5x5 System
- **Structure**: 5 sets of 5 repetitions for major compound movements
- **Progression**: Gradual increase in load over time
- **Foundation**: Based on progressive overload principles

### Sample Workout - "Miss Universe Bulk Course"
- **Frequency**: Never more than 3 times per week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday)
- **Structure**: Progressive volume over 3-month cycle
  - Month 1: 3 sets per exercise
  - Month 2: 4 sets per exercise
  - Month 3: 5 sets per exercise

### Key Exercises and Rep Ranges
1. Barbell squat - Never above 5 reps
2. Breathing pullover - Set of 10 (not to failure)
3. Wide grip bench press - Never above 5 reps (sometimes as low as 2)
4. Power deadlift (rack pull) - Sets of 5
5. Barbell vertical shrug - As many reps as possible with max deadlift weight (cap at 20)
6. Wide grip bent over barbell row - Never above 5 reps
7. Barbell cheating curl - Heavy, never above 5 reps

## Physiological Insights
- Used heavy loads (3-5 rep range) which produce minimal post-workout fatigue
- Recovery allowed training 3x per week despite high set volumes
- Emphasized progressive overload with clear rep targets
- Warm-up: Explicitly recommended 2 warm-up sets before 5x5 work sets

## Significance
- Demonstrated effectiveness of heavy load training for hypertrophy
- Showed that heavy loads (up to 5 reps) produce less fatigue than moderate loads
- Repped up the 5x5 system still used today
- Example of pre-steroid era high-volume heavy-load programming being sustainable
