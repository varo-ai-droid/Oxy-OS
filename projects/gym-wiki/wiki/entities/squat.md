# Squat

## Description
A compound lower body exercise where the lifter descends into a squat position and returns to standing. The squat is a fundamental movement pattern that targets the entire lower body with emphasis on the quadriceps and glutes.

## Variations
- [[Barbell Hack Squat]] - Rear-foot elevated barbell squat variation invented by George Hackenschmidt
- Front squat - More quad-dominant, requires greater core stability
- High bar vs low bar - Bar position changes the mechanical demands on the posterior chain

## Historical Context
- One of the oldest and most fundamental strength training exercises
- Featured in silver era bodybuilding programs as a core lower body movement
- Early bodybuilders recognized its value for overall lower body development
