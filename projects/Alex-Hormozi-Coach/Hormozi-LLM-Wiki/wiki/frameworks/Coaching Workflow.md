# Coaching Workflow

Alex Hormozi's systematic coaching cadence for diagnosing, prescribing, and scaling business fixes.

## The Seven-Step Loop

1. **DIAGNOSE** — Review numbers, identify single biggest constraint
2. **PRESCRIBE** — Fix offer first using [[Value Equation]] + [[Grand Slam Offer]]
3. **SEQUENCE** — Apply [[Money Models]] in constraint order
4. **SPRINT** — 7-day implementation sprint with binary metric
5. **REVIEW** — Weekly check, score hit or miss
6. **ITERATE** — If miss, adjust offer/funnel. If hit, identify next constraint.
7. **SCALE** — Apply [[Core Four]] lead generation once model is proven

---

## Phase Details

### 1. Diagnose

Before prescribing anything, Hormozi reviews:
- Current business numbers (revenue, profit, lead volume, close rate)
- Single biggest constraint in the [[Money Models]] chain
- Client's stated dream outcome for the next 90 days
- Whether current [[Grand Slam Offer|offer]] can deliver that outcome

### 2. Prescribe

Once constraint is identified:
- Define or fix the [[Grand Slam Offer]]
- Sequence the [[Money Models]] layers to fix the constraint
- Set a 7-day sprint to implement
- Define the metric that proves it's working

### 3. Accountability Loop

Weekly metric review using same 3-5 numbers:
- Binary scoring: metric either hits target or doesn't
- Miss: diagnose why, adjust offer/funnel, run again next week
- Hit: ask what broke or what will break next, then scale

---

## Key Principles

- **Fix the offer first** — A great offer compensates for mediocre execution
- **One constraint at a time** — Business is only as strong as its weakest link
- **7-day sprints** — Timebox all interventions, measure in one week or less
- **Eliminate optionality** — Kill all side projects until core business works
- **Direct feedback** — "You have a [X] problem, not a [Y] problem"

---

## Laser vs Lamp Thinking

> **"Most people don't need to work harder. They need to think clearer."**

Focus is everything. A laser burns a hole through a wall; a lamp scatters light across a room. Entrepreneurs who break through revenue ceilings get ruthlessly clear on the one direction that matters and say no to everything else.

---

## Listening Training

When coaching clients on communication, Hormozi teaches:
- Nodding when people speak
- Adding proper "ahhs" or "oohs" when someone speaks
- Reciting back what they said in fewer words
