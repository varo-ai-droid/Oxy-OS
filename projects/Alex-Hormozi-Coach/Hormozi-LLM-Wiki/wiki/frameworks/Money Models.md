# Money Models

## Definition
A **Money Model** is the strategy for monetizing an offer. It determines how you get paid, when you get paid, and what you get paid for. Money models are the engine that converts customers into revenue.

## The 3 Stages of Money Models

### Stage 1: Acquisition
Getting customers through any means - advertising, marketing, referrals, etc.

### Stage 2: Monetization
Making money from customers through various monetization strategies.

### Stage 3: Retention
Keeping customers longer to maximize lifetime value.

## The Four Categories of Monetization

### 1. Attraction Offers
Strategies that get prospects to notice and engage with your offer.

**Strategies:**
- Standard attraction offer - compelling initial price/value
- Win your money back offer - risk reversal as the primary attractor
- Giveaway offers - free or heavily subsidized initial offer
- Decoy offer - using a strategically inferior option to drive purchases
- Buy X Get Y Free offers - bundling to increase perceived value

### 2. Upsell Offers
Strategies to increase the value of each customer transaction.

**Strategies:**
- Classic upsell - simple upgrade path
- Menu upsell - multiple upgrade options presented
- A/B upsell - two distinct upgrade paths
- Uncertainty upsell - reduces risk while adding value
- Prescription upsell - personalized recommendation for next steps
- Stacking upsells - multiple sequential upsells
- Anchor upsell - using a high anchor to make middle option attractive
- Rollover upsell - unused value rolls into next period

### 3. Downsell Offers
Strategies for prospects who decline initial offers.

**Strategies:**
- Payment plan downsell - spread payments over time
- Trial with penalty downsell - limited access with consequences
- Feature downsell - reduced feature set at lower price

### 4. Continuity Offers
Recurring revenue models that create predictable income.

**Strategies:**
- Standard continuity - subscription/recurring models
- Continuity with bonuses - recurring with added incentives
- Discounted continuity - recurring at special rates
- Waived fee continuity - initial fees waived for recurring

## Key Principle
Money models should make you money while acquiring customers, not after. The best models get you paid immediately upon customer acquisition, removing cash constraints on growth.
