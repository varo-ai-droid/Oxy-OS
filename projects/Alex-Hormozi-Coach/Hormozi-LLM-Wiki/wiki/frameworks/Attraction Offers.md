# Attraction Offers

## Definition
**Attraction Offers** are strategies that get prospects to notice and engage with your offer. They form the foundation of your monetization strategy by drawing customers in with compelling initial value propositions.

## The 5 Types of Attraction Offers

### 1. Standard Attraction Offer
**Compelling initial price/value that stands out in the marketplace.**

Create an offer so differentiated that prospects cannot compare it to alternatives. Focus on value density - getting them more value for their dollar than they expect.

*Strategy: Make the offer itself the primary attractor through superior value proposition.*

### 2. Win Your Money Back Offer
**Risk reversal as the primary attractor.**

Offer full or partial refund if customers don't achieve specific results. Removes perceived risk and shifts the decision from "should I buy?" to "why wouldn't I try this?"

*Example: "If you don't lose 20 pounds in 6 weeks, we'll refund your money plus your grocery costs."*

### 3. Giveaway Offers
**Free or heavily subsidized initial offer.**

Provide substantial value for free or at a token cost to attract prospects. Use as a loss leader to build trust and demonstrate value before monetizing.

*Examples:*
- Free strategy session
- Free trial
- Free sample with shipping covered
- Free presentations - educational content as primary attractor
- Freemium - partial service offered for free with paid upgrade paths
- Pick Your Price - customer chooses their price point, removing purchase barriers

### 4. Decoy Offer
**Using a strategically inferior option to drive purchases.**

Create a deliberately unattractive option that makes your main offer look better by comparison. The decoy should be obviously inferior to avoid cannibalization.

*Example: Premium plan at $997, Standard at $497, and Basic (decoy) at $397 with minimal features - pushes toward Standard.*

### 5. Buy X Get Y Free Offers
**Bundling to increase perceived value.**

Offer additional items or services at no extra cost. Creates perception of getting a deal while increasing the total value package.

*Example: "Buy one month, get two weeks free" or "Sign up today, get our premium course bundle free."*

## Key Principle
Attraction offers should attract the type of customer you want. Be careful not to attract bargain hunters who will never upgrade or become long-term customers.
