# Bonuses

## Definition
Bonuses are additional items given *on top* of the core offer to make it more appealing without lowering the price. They increase the prospect's price-to-value discrepancy until it's irresistible.

## Core Principle
When prospects ask for a discount, *add more value* through bonuses instead of cutting the price. Never discount the main offer - it teaches customers prices are negotiable.

## Value Perception & Stacking

### The Stacking Effect
A single offer is *less valuable* than the same offer broken into component parts and stacked as bonuses. This subconsciously communicates that the core offer must be valuable if the bonuses are so good.

### One-on-One Selling Strategy
1. Ask for the sale first
2. If they say "yes," reveal additional bonuses *after* they've signed up to create a "wow" experience and reinforce their decision
3. If they say "no," present a bonus that matches their perceived obstacle, then ask again. Leverage reciprocity

## Bonus Bullets Framework

### 11-Point Checklist for Bonus Creation

1. **Always Offer Them:** Use a pre-prepared "bulleted bundle" of 3-5 high-value items
2. **Name They Specifically:** Use benefit-driven titles that resonate with the prospect
3. **Explain Each Bonus:**
   - How it relates to their issue
   - What it is exactly
   - How it was discovered/created
   - How it improves their life (faster, easier, less effort)
4. **Provide Proof:** Stats, client testimonials, personal experience
5. **Paint a Mental Image:** Describe their life *after* using it
6. **Ascribe a Price Tag:** Even if "free" - gives it perceived value
7. **Prioritize Tools/Checklists:** Lower effort for higher perceived value than additional training
8. **Address Specific Concerns:** Prove their negative belief incorrect
9. **Solve Their "Next Problem":** What they'll logically need after the core offer
10. **Psychological Value:** Bonuses should eclipse the core offer's value
11. **Enhance with Scarcity & Urgency:**
    - Scarcity: "Only people who sign up get access to Bonus X"
    - Urgency: "If you buy today, I will add in XYZ bonus for free"

## Advanced Bonus Techniques

### Other Businesses' Products/Services
- Get non-competing businesses to provide their services as bonuses
- Exchange: Exposure to your clients (free marketing for them)
- **Monetization:** Negotiate group discounts and commission for yourself - turning bonuses into indirect revenue streams

## Bonus Vault Strategy

### Summary Checklist:
1. Create reusable, high-value content: checklists, tools, swipe files, scripts, templates
2. Record all workshops, webinars, events, interviews
3. Use content to crush perceived obstacles
4. Proactively negotiate group discounts with adjacent businesses

**Best Bonus Types:** Information products, virtual events, or fixed-cost VIP services work well as they provide high perceived value.
