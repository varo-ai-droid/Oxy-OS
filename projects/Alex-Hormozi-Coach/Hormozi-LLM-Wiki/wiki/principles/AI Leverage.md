# AI Leverage

Alex Hormozi's approach to using AI as leverage to multiply output rather than replace human workers. His philosophy: **use AI to replace inefficiency, not headcount.**

## Core Philosophy

AI should be used to:
- Eliminate time-consuming manual tasks
- Increase productivity across teams (reports of up to 40% improvement in some areas)
- Free up time for human-centric work requiring creativity and judgment
- Scale operations without proportional headcount increases

## Key Benefits

- **Productivity Gains:** 25-40% improvements across different functions
- **Time Savings:** Sales team saves ~25% of work week (equivalent to 1 full day)
- **Scalability:** Scale without proportional headcount increases
- **Human Focus:** AI handles repetitive tasks, freeing humans for creative/strategic work

## AI Implementation by Function

### Sales (25% Time Savings)
- **Personalized Video Outreach:** Record one base video, AI personalizes with prospect names and context via Lemlist
- **Email Automation:** AI-drafted follow-up emails after sales calls (Compose AI)
- **Result:** ~25% of work week saved for actual sales conversations

### Content Creation
- **Audio Generation:** Resemble AI creates lifelike audio content without re-recording
- **Video/Audio Editing:** 
  - Runway automates image editing tasks (object removal, background replacement)
  - Descript enables keyword search to find specific moments in recordings
- **Result:** Massive reduction in post-production time

### Training & Onboarding
- **AI-Generated Training Videos:** Colossyan creates onboarding videos with avatars; updates via text changes
- **Result:** Hundreds of hours saved, instant training material updates

### Recruiting & Talent Acquisition
- **Digital Twin Outreach:** Tavus sends personalized messages to passive top performers
- **Interview Automation:** Otter.ai captures interview notes with timestamps
- **Result:** More efficient talent acquisition, better candidate experience

### Operations & Meetings
- **Calendar Optimization:** Reclaim AI reshuffles meetings to minimize gaps (up to 40% time savings)
- **Meeting Documentation:** Fireflies.ai automatically takes notes and creates summaries
- **Result:** 40% reduction in wasted calendar time, thousands of scheduling emails eliminated
