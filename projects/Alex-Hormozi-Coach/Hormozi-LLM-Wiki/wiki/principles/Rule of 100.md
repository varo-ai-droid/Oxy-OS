# Rule of 100

## Definition
The **Rule of 100** is Alex Hormozi's principle for guaranteeing more engaged leads through consistent daily action. Commit to 100 primary actions per day for 100 days straight.

## Core Principle
Volume and consistency compound to create predictable lead generation results. 100 days × 100 actions = 10,000 total actions that build momentum and expertise.

## The Three Lead Generation Applications

### Content Creation
- **Action:** Release at least **1 piece of content per day** on a platform
- **Purpose:** Build organic audience and authority
- **Cumulative Effect:** 100 days = ~100 pieces of content

### Cold Outreach
- **Action:** Perform **100 reachouts per day**
- **Methods:** Email, text, DM, cold call, flyers
- **Purpose:** Direct lead generation through personal outreach
- **Cumulative Effect:** 100 days = ~10,000 direct contacts

### Paid Ads
- **Action:** Spend **100 minutes per day** creating and managing ads
- **Purpose:** Systematic ad optimization and scaling
- **Cumulative Effect:** 100 days = ~166 hours of focused ad work

## Key Insights

### Consistency Trumps Perfection
The goal is not perfection but sustained daily action. Skills develop through repetition and feedback.

### Momentum Building
Day after day of action creates:
- Increased response rates
- Better messaging through testing
- Refined targeting and offers
- Stronger sales skills

### Skill Acquisition
Just like the "10,000 hour rule" for mastery, 10,000 actions create:
- Expertise in outreach methods
- Intuition for what works
- Confidence in sales conversations
- Systems that scale

## Implementation Tips

### Start Small, Think Big
You don't need to do all three methods - pick one and apply the Rule of 100.

### Track Everything
Log all 100 actions daily to identify patterns and improvements.

### Focus on Constraints
After 30-40 days, you'll identify where to focus for maximum improvement.
