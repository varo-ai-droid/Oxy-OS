# Glossary

## Core Frameworks

**Grand Slam Offer** - An offer that cannot be compared to any other because it combines attractive promotion, unmatchable value proposition, premium price, unbeatable guarantee, and a money model that gets you paid to acquire customers.

**Value Equation** - `(Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice) = Value` - The fundamental equation for understanding perceived value.

**Money Models** - The four ways to monetize: Attraction, Upsell, Downsell, and Continuity offers. Each layer compounds the others.

**Core Four** - The four essential lead generation methods: Warm Reachouts, Content Posting, Cold Reachouts, and Paid Ads.

**MOSY 6 Framework** - Diagnostic tool for business constraints: Market, Offer, Sales & Traffic, Systems, Yield & People, Operations.

## Offer Creation Terms

**Dream Outcome** - What the prospect ultimately wants to achieve - their ideal end state, not the product features.

**Done-For-You (DFY)** - Service delivery where the provider executes everything on behalf of the customer.

**Do-With-You (DWY)** - Service delivery where the provider teaches/guides the customer through the process.

**Do-It-Yourself (DIY)** - Service delivery where the customer figures it out on their own.

**MAGIC Naming** - M (Magnet) - A (Avatar) - G (Goal) - I (Interval) - C (Container) formula for creating compelling offer names.

## Delivery Terms

**Delivery Cube** - Six dimensions for brainstorming delivery methods: Personal Attention, Effort Expected, Medium, Consumption, Speed, and 10x/1/10th Stretch Test.

**10x to 1/10th Stretch Test** - Asking "If customers paid 10x, what would you provide? If they paid 1/10th, what would you provide?" to create tiered offerings.

## Growth Frameworks

**Scale vs Skill** - Framework for deciding when to improve personal abilities vs. building systems and hiring.

**Three Ways to Increase Revenue** - Price, Volume, and Frequency/Value per Customer are the only three levers to grow any business.

**Rule of 100** - 100 actions per day for 100 days to build expertise and predictable results.

## Customer Terms

**Starving Crowd** - A market with massive, urgent demand that desperately needs your solution.

**Customer Avatar** - Detailed profile of your ideal customer including demographics, psychographics, and behavioral patterns.

**Rendezvous Point** - Where customer and business interests align perfectly - mutual benefit with no trade-offs.

**Perceived Likelihood of Achievement** - Customer's belief that they will actually achieve their desired outcome.

## Monetization Terms

**Customer Financed Acquisition (CFA)** - Using customer revenue to fund your customer acquisition activities. Three levers: CAC, LTGP, PPD.

**CAC** - Cost to Acquire a Customer - the expense of getting one new customer.

**LTGP** - Lifetime Gross Profit - total profit received from a customer over their entire relationship.

**PPD** - Payback Period - time between acquisition cost and recouping that investment.

## Offer Enhancers

**Scarcity** - Limiting quantity to increase perceived value through fear of missing out.

**Urgency** - Time-based limitations to spur immediate action.

**Bonuses** - Additional items that increase the offer's perceived value.

**Guarantees** - Risk reversal strategies that make people feel stupid saying no.

## Mindset & Philosophy

**Laser vs Lamp Thinking** - Focus on one direction vs. spreading attention across many things.

**Constraint Theory** - Identify and fix the single biggest constraint before adding resources.

**Perception Equals Reality** - In offer crafting, what customers perceive is what matters, not the objective truth.

## Team & Scaling

**What-Why-How Framework** - Diagnostic for underperforming employees: What (communication), How (training), Why (motivation).
