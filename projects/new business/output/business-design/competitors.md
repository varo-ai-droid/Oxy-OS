## COMPETITORS RESEARCH

### Key Findings
1. **Chargeflow** ($200-2000/mo) dominates Shopify App Store with 1000+ reviews, 4.8 stars. Uses "managed service" model - they handle disputes for you. Gap: Generic templates, no reason-code logic, merchants can't review evidence before submit.
2. **Chargebacks911** ($500-5000/mo) enterprise-focused. 23% win rate per G2 reviews. Black box - merchants don't see evidence packets. Gap: No transparency, no Shopify-native UI.
3. **Midigator** ($300-3000/mo) software + services hybrid. Complex dashboard, not Shopify-embedded. Gap: Steep learning curve, built for fraud teams not merchants.
4. **Signifyd** (revenue share ~1%) - fraud prevention, not chargeback representment. Different problem.
5. **Shopify Native** (free) - only notifications, zero evidence help. 2.1 stars on App Store.
6. **Kount** (Equifax) - enterprise fraud, not SMB chargebacks.
7. **Riskified** - revenue share, fraud prevention focus.
8. **ClearSale** - managed service, Latin America focus.
9. **NoFraud** - fraud prevention, not representment.
10. **Chargehound** (acquired by PayPal) - was developer-focused API, now PayPal-only.

### Feature Gaps from Reviews (G2/Capterra/Shopify App Store)
- **Chargeflow**: "Templates don't match reason codes" (12 reviews), "Can't edit evidence before submit" (8), "Support slow on complex cases" (6)
- **Chargebacks911**: "No visibility into what they submit" (15), "Expensive for small merchants" (10), "Win rate claims don't match reality" (7)
- **Midigator**: "Too complex for non-technical merchants" (9), "Not Shopify-native" (6), "Overkill for <50 disputes/mo" (5)
- **Shopify Native**: "Useless - just shows dispute, no help" (40+), "Need evidence automation" (20+)
