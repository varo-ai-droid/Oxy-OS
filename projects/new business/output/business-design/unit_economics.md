## UNIT_ECONOMICS RESEARCH

### Key Findings
1. **CAC by Channel** (B2B SaaS, Shopify App Store):
   - Shopify App Store SEO: $50-150 (organic, high intent)
   - Content/SEO: $100-300 (case studies, reason-code guides)
   - Agency Partnerships: $200-500 (rev share 20-30%, longer cycle)
   - Paid Search (Google): $300-800 (high competition "chargeback")
   - Paid Social (Twitter/LinkedIn): $400-1000 (niche targeting)
   - Outbound: $500-1500 (high touch, enterprise only)

2. **Pricing Model Validation**:
   - $19/response (usage): Low friction, aligns with merchant success
   - $99/mo + $9/response (Pro): Predictable for 20+/mo merchants
   - $499/mo (Enterprise): Multi-store, ERP sync, dedicated support

3. **Churn Benchmarks** (B2B SaaS, <$100/mo):
   - Monthly logo churn: 3-5% (good), 5-7% (average), >7% (concerning)
   - Annual revenue churn: 20-30% (net negative churn possible with expansion)
   - Shopify app churn: Higher (2-3% monthly) due to store closures

4. **Support Costs**:
   - Tier 1 (self-serve): $0.50/ticket (docs, chatbot)
   - Tier 2 (email): $15-25/ticket
   - Tier 3 (zoom/phone): $75-150/ticket
   - Target: 80% self-serve, 15% email, 5% calls

5. **Gross Margins at Scale**:
   - Infrastructure: 5-8% (API calls, PDF generation, hosting)
   - Support: 10-15% at scale
   - Payment processing (Shopify Billing): 15-20%
   - Gross margin: 65-75% (SaaS benchmark 70-80%)

### Recommendations
1. **Launch at $19/response** - lowest friction, expands naturally
2. **Add Pro at $99/mo** by Month 3 (merchants ask for predictability)
3. **Enterprise at $499** only when 3+ logos request it
4. **Target 3% monthly churn** - invest in onboarding (first 7 days critical)
5. **Net negative churn via expansion** - build upgrade triggers (volume, features)
6. **Shopify App Store = primary channel** - invest in SEO, reviews, case studies
7. **Agency channel** - 20% rev share, target top 50 Shopify Plus agencies

### Risks
- Shopify take rate increase (15% → 20% → 25%)
- Chargeflow drops price to $9/response
- Store closure churn (uncontrollable, ~1-2%/mo)
- API cost increases (Gmail, shipping carriers)
