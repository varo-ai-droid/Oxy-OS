## Production Notes

**Visual Style**: Screen-recorded UI (real product), clean motion graphics for evidence graph, merchant b-roll (stock or user-submitted)
**Voiceover**: Nate (founder) — conversational, not announcer
**Music**: 
- 0-10s: Low tension drone (sub-bass, slow pulse)
- 10-45s: Upbeat, modern SaaS beat (120-130 BPM, pluck synth)
- 45-60s: Warm pads, subtle rhythm
- 60-90s: Fade to calm ambient

**SFX**: 
- Notification ping (0:00)
- Stamp slam (0:04)
- Keyboard typing (0:14)
- Node connections popping (0:18)
- Confetti pop (0:24)
- Cash register ka-ching (subtle, 0:35)

**Captions**: Burned-in, high contrast, animated word-by-word for TikTok/Reels
**Aspect Ratios**: 9:16 (primary), 16:9 (website), 1:1 (Instagram feed)
