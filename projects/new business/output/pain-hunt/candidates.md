## Candidate 1: Chargeback Evidence Automation for Shopify
**Core Problem**: Merchants lose winnable chargebacks because evidence compilation is manual, scattered across tools (Shopify, email, shipping, ERP), and generic templates don't match reason code requirements.

**Supporting Problems**: #1, #2, #3, #4, #7, #8, #22
**Sources**: Reddit (3), G2, Shopify Forums, IndieHackers, Twitter
