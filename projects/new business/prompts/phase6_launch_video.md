## Your Task
Write a 60-90 second launch video script for TikTok/Reels/Shorts + website.

### Structure
| Time | Section | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| 0-3s | HOOK | Visceral problem moment |
| 3-10s | PAIN | Current solutions fail |
| 10-25s | REVEAL | Product demo - show, don't tell |
| 25-45s | PROOF | Social proof, metrics, "built for you" |
| 45-60s | OFFER | Price, guarantee, CTA |
| 60-90s | FOUNDER | Optional personal note |

### Format
```
[VISUAL]                          | [AUDIO/VOICEOVER]
----------------------------------|------------------
Screen recording: chargeback email| "A chargeback just hit your store."
Dashboard: $2,847 lost            | "The bank believes the customer."
                                    | "Your tool? Sent a template."
Product demo: evidence builder    | "CounterBrief builds the case."
                                    | "Tracking. Emails. Order history."
                                    | "Assembled. You approve. $19."
Split screen: template vs Counter | "Templates lose. Evidence wins."
Founder on camera                 | "I built this because I lost $40k."
                                    | "19 bucks. Keep every dollar you win."
Logo + URL                        | "CounterBrief.com"
```

### Requirements
- Visual column: specific enough for editor/animator
- Audio column: conversational, punchy, <150 words/minute
- Include music cues, SFX suggestions
- Mark sections for: viral cut (0-15s), demo cut (15-45s), founder cut (60-90s)
