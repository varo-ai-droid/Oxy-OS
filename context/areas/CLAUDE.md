# This Area: Life + Business Domains

## What Lives Here
Areas of life and work that need structured context and rules. Currently includes:
- Business/projects
- Personal development
- Learning/skill building
- Health/fitness (your private passion)

## Rules That Only Apply Here
- **Business area:** Focus on shipping. Every idea must have a concrete next action, not just theory.
- **Learning area:** Track progress visibly. Skills without shippable output are just research.
- **Fitness area:** Your private hypertrophy/bodybuilding knowledge — treat as confidential unless you explicitly share.
- **All areas:** Always tie back to your core question — "What would make Oxy more effective/efficient?"
